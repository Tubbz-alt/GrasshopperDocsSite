gHowl

ADDON. Version 0.1.r50.0. Released on 2014-Nov-21. Provides 15 components. Created by Damien Alomar, Luis Fraguada and Giulio Piacentino, in alphabetical order. Has 1 video tutorials.
gHowl is a set of interoperability components which extends the ability of Grasshopper to communicate with other applications.

Peregrine

ADDON, Version 2019-Nov-19, Released on 2019-Nov-19, Provides 27 components, Created by LimitState 3D
Peregrine is a LimitState:FORM powered optimization plugin for Grasshopper. Quickly define and optimize your designs within Grasshopper, while making use of a wide array of powerful editing and rationalization functions based on LimitState:FORM technology.

DistanceDThe list of distanced to use in the construction of the new internal vertices. One distance/vertexNumber
OffsetTypeTDefinition of distance for new vertices

0: Distance On Diagonals. The measurement on the diagonal decides the offset.
1: Hypotenuse Average. Trigonometrical. This setting is experimental..
2: Planes Intersections. Planes intersections, works best with valence-three vertices. This setting is experimental..		OffsetTypeEnum

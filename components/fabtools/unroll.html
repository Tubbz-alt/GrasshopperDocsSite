Unroll

Component Index FabToolsUtilsUnroll

Unrolls Breps and Surfaces with additional curves and points.
by blickfeld7.com

Inputs

NameIDDescriptionType
Poly SurfaceBThe Brep to unroll.Brep
PointsPPoints to unroll with the surfaces.Point
CurvesCThe curves to unroll with the surfaces.Curve
Frame offsetoffThe offset of the frames on the construction plane.Number
FramefThe Frame to place the unrolled geometries ontoRectangle
AlignmentalignThe alignment of the geometry in the frames on construction plane.String

Outputs

NameIDDescriptionType
SurfaceSThe unrolled Surfaces.Surface
CurveCThe unrolled curves.Curve
PointPThe points on the unrolled surface.Point
FramefThe frames of the unrolled surfaces.Rectangle

Decode Phenotype

Component Index WallaceiWallacei XPDecoder

This component decodes the Wallacei phenotypes outputted by the WallaceiX component into three outputs – Geometry (points/curves/surfaces/breps), Meshes and Numbers.

Inputs

NameIDDescriptionType
Wallacei PhenotypeWPhenotypesInsert the phenotypes outputted by the WallaceiX component.Goo

Outputs

NameIDDescriptionType
Geometries ListGeometriesOutputs the Geometries for the exported solutions.GeometricGoo
Meshes ListMeshesOutputs the Meshes for the exported solutions.Mesh
Numbers ListNumbersOutputs the Numbers for the exported solutions.Number
Text ListTextOutputs the Text for the exported solutions.String

Gene Pool and Slider shuffler

Component Index WallaceiWallacei UtilitiesShuffler

This component shuffles the gene pools and number sliders on the grasshopper canvas that have been renamed with wlc_ prefix. This helps users shuffle the values in the gene pools and sliders in order to quickly visualize their effect on the geometry they control.

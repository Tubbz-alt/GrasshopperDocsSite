Select Mesh Edge

Component Index > MeshPlus > Select > m+SelEdge

Allows for the selection of Grasshopper or Rhino Mesh Edges. Returning the Indices, Mid Point, and Representational Line at each selection.
(Note: Grasshopper geometry is made selectable by temporarily baking it to Rhino to acquire the topology information.)
